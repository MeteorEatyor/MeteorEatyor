Utilizing Loss Adversion to Create Profitable Low Development Cost Online Experiences.

People are naturally loss adverse, this needs no citation. [1]
1.Proven by Epic Games
Fortnite has perfected the art of creating a simple gameplay loop with hihgly profitable monetization practices. The idea is to create optional elements in your application, often cosmetics that have a level of artificial scarcity. This can be completed with ingame shops having rotating catalogues, limited time promotional items, and it can even be as simple as making an item a limited quantity.
2.No Unreal Required: Low Development Costs
Many mobile experiences already exist which do not utilize the proper makings of a "game." This excesises the implied development of a complex gameplay loop. Development costs can be kept low as long as charcaters are appealing to a general audience. Ideally accquire any IP, but can be done with effective market research during the design phase of an original IP.
3.The Pull of Gacha Systems
Further profit optimization of the rotating digital storefront model can be achieved through using "Gacha" elements. Where individual item(s) are not offered, but instead the chance to draw from a pool of many different items, encouraging repeated pulls to get the desired outcome. This drives down development costs by making rare content lasting and scarce.
